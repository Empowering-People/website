Who are our services for?

Our processes are both professional and simple

We will carry out a complete assessment of the available roles & workplaces

1


Our dedicated mentors will undertake an in-depth interview with each possible candidate to fully understand their disabilities, ambition and capabilities.


2


We will then work to find the perfect employment/role placement and only once both employer & employee are happy, will we agree on the required support and commence the funding application.


3


A tailored support plan will be developed specific to the candidate's needs and ensure a job coach is in place that understands both the candidate and the process, holding regular meetings with both parties to ensure development is on track and the candidate's welfare is being prioritised.
