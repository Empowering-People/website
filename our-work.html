Our Goals

To enable individuals with hidden disabilities to have independence, find employment, and empower them to live their best life.

To help people and organisations fully understand what it means to work as an individual or employ an individual with a hidden disability.

Our Promise

As an organisation with a focused goal of supporting adults with learning and social disabilities, our promise is to help people make their way in championing an independent and fulfilling life.

We deliver this promise through bespoke plans, tailored to an individual's specific needs, helping them to define what independence looks like for them, while working to understand their personal goals, ambitions, and enable them to achieve the best possible chance of success.

Our Area of Expertise

  • Work Placement & Recruitment
  • Job Coaching
  • Arranging Funding (Access to Work)
  • Personal Mentoring
  • Life Coaching
  • All Aspects of Application Process
