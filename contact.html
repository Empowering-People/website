To contact Empowering People, please send an email to: info@empowering-people.co.uk
Or phone: 0141-763-6668

If you've noticed a problem with our website, whether it be a simple typo, or an accessibility shortfall, or something completely different, please don't inform us of it by email or phone, instead submit an issue to our website's GitHub repository. This allows us to better keep track of these things, and substantially lessens the likelihood that your concerns get lost among other emails we receive.
